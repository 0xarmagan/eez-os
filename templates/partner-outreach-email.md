# Partner Outreach Email Template

The two-asks pattern. Used for EEZ launch and major milestones. Adapt the asks for the moment.

---

**Subject:** EEZ launch — two quick asks

Hey [Name],

We're [N] days out from the EEZ announcement at [event] — going live [date] from [time]. Two things that would mean a lot:

**1. Brand account ([date], after [time])** — QT the announcement from @EthEconomicZone confirming you're part of the alliance. Your own words, but something like: *"[Project] is supporting the EEZ initiative — EEZ liquidity, composability, security."*

**2. Personal note ([date range])** — A few sentences from you or a founder on why EEZ matters and what you're building toward. Doesn't need to be promotional, just your honest take on where Ethereum needs to go.

Full comms framework and example posts [here]([link]).

Also — are you going to be in [location]? We're recording short interviews at the EEZ booth and would love to get you on camera if so.

Hold everything until the [date] and we'll tag you in the announcement.

Ben

---

## Adaptations

- **For new partners**: replace asks with the alliance overview + Tally form + offer of a kickoff call.
- **For drift recovery**: lead with a check-in question, not an ask. Reference the partner's recent work first.
- **For technical spec partners**: include a direct line to the EEZ tech leads or a calendar link for a working session.

## Voice rules

- No em dashes
- No "Hi" formal opener; use "Hey" or first name
- Sign as "Ben", not "Best regards, Ben"
- Hold the substance up front; ask at the end
