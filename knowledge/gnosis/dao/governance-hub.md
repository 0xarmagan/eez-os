# GnosisDAO Governance Hub

The canonical reference for how GnosisDAO governance works.

## Channels

- **Forum:** forum.gnosis.io (Discourse). Hosts proposal discussions and GIP drafts.
- **Snapshot:** formal off-chain voting for gnosis.eth.
- **On-chain governance:** GNO token, 75,000 GNO quorum threshold.
- **Discord:** primary community hub.
- **X/Twitter:** @GnosisDAO, plus team accounts (@gaborish, @gaborGnosisPay, @koeppelmann, @aboutcircles).
- **Ghost blogs:** gnosis.ghost.io and gnosisdao.ghost.io for weekly recaps and governance summaries.

## Delegate program

10 delegates with 5,000 GNO each. Coordinated by StableLab and karpatkey.

## GIP lifecycle

1. **Idea** — surfaced on forum, Discord, or in a working group
2. **Draft** — formalised on the forum following the GIP template (see `skills/gip-authoring/SKILL.md`)
3. **Phase 1: Discussion** — community comments, refinements
4. **Phase 2: Temperature check** — informal poll on the forum
5. **Phase 3: Snapshot vote** — formal off-chain vote
6. **On-chain execution** — for GIPs requiring on-chain action, executed via the relevant Safe / governance contracts

## Engagement principles

- The forum is canonical. Decisions reference forum threads.
- Multi-channel notification is required for high-importance items (forum + Discord + Telegram + X).
- Conflict-of-interest disclosure is mandatory for parties with Gnosis affiliation voting on funded projects.
- Audit trail is permanent and public.
