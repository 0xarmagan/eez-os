## Elevator pitch

The most resilient, secure, and credibly neutral EVM sidechain. Offering immediate utility and ultra-low transaction costs. Onboarding the world's money via a network of local stablecoins.

## Short description

Gnosis Chain is an EVM-equivalent L1 secured by 125k+ validators, designed as Ethereum's companion chain for stable, low-cost, payment-grade transactions. Native token: GNO. Gas token: xDAI.

## Key differentiators

- 125k+ validators, among the most decentralised PoS networks
- Sub-cent transaction costs
- Zero downtime since 2018
- Stable gas via xDAI
- Ethereum-equivalent EVM, no migration friction
- Local stablecoin issuance roadmap
