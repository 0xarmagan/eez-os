## Elevator pitch

A VPN where no single party — including the operator — can identify the user. Requests are relayed via multiple HOPR nodes; even the exit node cannot see your IP. No fees, no centralised control, no proprietary ownership.

## Short description

Built on HOPR's mixnet, Gnosis Chain settlement, and Circles v2 identity. Unlike traditional VPN providers, value accrues to node runners and contributors rather than a corporate entity.

## How it differs from a regular VPN

- Multi-node relay so no single entity can identify the source
- Even the exit node cannot see your IP address
- No fees, no centralised control
- Value accrues to node runners and contributors
- Standalone legal entity (Seldon Inc, per GIP-129)
