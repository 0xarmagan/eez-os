## Elevator pitch

Run an onchain business like a regular business. Stablecoin payments, expense management, accounting integration. Self-custodial throughout.

## Short description

A business banking suite for the onchain economy. Self-custodial business accounts supporting stablecoin payments, batch payment processing with maker-checker approval, invoice generation, Visa debit cards for real-world expenses, and integration with Xero and QuickBooks for crypto cost basis, P&L, and balance reporting.

Acquired (as HQ.xyz) for $15M in May 2025 and rebranded as Gnosis Business.

## Key differentiators

- Self-custodial (not custodial like Mercury or competitors)
- Stablecoin-native, with off-ramp to bank accounts
- Maker-checker approval workflows for batch payments
- Xero / QuickBooks integration for accounting
- Crypto cost basis and P&L reporting
