## Elevator pitch

The consumer-facing mobile application unifying the Gnosis product suite. Self-custodial wallet, free virtual Visa card, IBAN for EUR deposits, Circles social tokens, and swaps in one app. No hidden fees, no FX fees, designed for non-crypto users.

## Short description

Consumer-facing mobile app bringing together the full Gnosis product ecosystem in one place. Self-custodial wallet for managing crypto across chains, free virtual Visa debit card with up to 5% cashback, IBAN account for depositing EUR from any local bank, native Circles social token integration, hourly rewards, exclusive partner deals, and built-in swaps. All with no hidden fees and zero FX costs.
