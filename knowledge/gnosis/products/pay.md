## Elevator pitch

Spend crypto anywhere Visa is accepted, directly from a self-custodial wallet. No off-ramp, no centralised custodian.

## Short description

A decentralised payment network and self-custodial crypto card that lets users spend digital assets at any Visa-accepting merchant. Unlike traditional crypto cards, Gnosis Pay does not require off-ramping through a centralised custodian; users spend directly from their own wallet.

## v2 — the major pivot

**Targeted launch:** end of June 2026.

A fundamental shift from consumer-facing payments (2023–2026) to fully developer-facing, B2B white-label infrastructure. Key moves:

- Multi-chain expansion. Gnosis Pay will no longer be Gnosis Chain-only, following Safe's multi-chain footprint.
- BRLA → USDC swap integration, bringing it close to Gnosis Ramps.
- Open-source front end, shipped with partners.
- Competitive positioning against RAIN (both competitor and partner).

## Key differentiators

- Self-custodial (key differentiator vs every centralised crypto card)
- Visa-compatible
- Multi-chain (post-v2)
- Developer APIs for white-label deployment
- Aligned with Gnosis Ramps for fiat-crypto bridging
