***Announcing the development of the Ethereum Economic Zone (EEZ), a maximally aligned Layer 2 ecosystem for Ethereum.*** 

*The Ethereum Economic Zone is being developed by Gnosis alongside Jordi Baylina, and with funding from the Ethereum Foundation and other key stakeholders.*

*Smart contract level composability with the Ethereum Mainnet enables any protocol in the EEZ to plug into shared infrastructure and liquidity from Ethereum, and the full security of the mainnet, out-of-the-box. Using ETH for gas.*

**Cannes, 29th March 2026** \- Gnosis, the EVM-aligned ecosystem powering secure asset management, decentralised trading, governance, and payments, today confirmed that they will play a key role in the development of a new Ethereum Layer 2 network, alongside Jordi Baylina and with funding from the Ethereum Foundation. 

Announced at EthCC, in Cannes, (INSERT SPECIFICS), this marks an important strategic initiative for Ethereum. The Ethereum Economic Zone acts as an alternative to a fragmented L2 landscape, allowing protocols to build with the true security of Ethereum, without having to rebuild core infrastructure like wallets and bridges, to compose seamlessly with other participating protocols, all while growing the utility of ETH as an ecosystem gas token. 

Gnosis has always been at the leading edge of Ethereum technology. Gnosis engineers were the first to develop constant product AMMs, which enabled contemporary DeFi including CoW Protocol. Gnosis also built the Conditional Token Framework, which is used by Polymarket. And long before ‘Account Abstraction’ became a core Ethereum roadmap priority, Gnosis developed the first production-grade implementation of smart contract wallets with Safe.

Dr Friederike Ernst, Co-Founder of Gnosis, said: “We’ve always moved in step with Ethereum \- helping lay the foundations of modern DeFi, and delivering core infrastructure like smart contract wallets with Safe well before account abstraction became a priority. We’re now carrying that same conviction into the next era. Rather than operating in isolation, this new framework is designed to work natively with Ethereum, sharing its security, liquidity, and core infrastructure.”

This work draws on Gnosis’ long-standing experience in protocol design and coordination mechanisms, and is being developed in active dialogue with the broader Ethereum research community, including contributors from the Ethereum Foundation.

**About Gnosis**  
Founded in 2015, Gnosis has evolved into a full-stack ecosystem bridging decentralised technology with everyday finance. Gnosis’ mission is to empower every person with greater financial agency and access, while supporting the creation of an open and collectively-owned financial system that benefits all. From its origins in decentralised prediction markets, Gnosis has grown into a full-spectrum provider spanning asset management, decentralised trading, and payments. Its industry-leading innovations include Safe, securing over $58 billion in assets; CoW Protocol, which has protected more than $130 billion in trades; and Gnosis Pay, enabling over $100m in payments to date, via crypto-native cards, for a growing ecosystem of wallets. Gnosis delivers a seamless, collectively-owned financial experience that puts accessibility and ownership at its core.
