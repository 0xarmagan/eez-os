# EEZ Alliance — Structure and Membership

## What the Alliance is

A workgroup. A collaborative space for organisations and people aligned with the EEZ vision to contribute to its development, community, and ultimately launch.

The intention: if we are to unify Ethereum, we need stakeholders from everywhere to support, and involving them early will support this.

## Membership scope

A collective of:
- EVM infrastructure providers
- Protocols
- Block builders
- Ecosystem facilitators

Currently **private to builders**. As things mature there will likely be a separate, more concentrated working group channel, but that is 3–6 months out.

## What the Alliance does

Four areas of contribution:
1. **Specification feedback**
2. **Technical contributions**
3. **User feedback or protocol-specific input**
4. **Early onboarding**

Coordination happens through gated community chats, community events, and activations around project milestones.

## How to join

Fill out the Tally form. If somebody in your network asks to join and you know them to be an operator somewhere in the EVM landscape, you can assume they will be welcomed, but please still ask them to fill out the form. Two brief questions on what they would like to contribute.

## What partners commit to

To be formalised in the Integration Guideline (in development by the Ecosystem Relations Lead). At minimum, founding members:
- Amplify the EEZ launch and key milestones via brand and personal accounts
- Provide protocol-specific feedback on the technical spec
- Make a senior contact available for monthly check-ins

## What EEZ delivers to partners

- Visibility in launch communications and ongoing milestones
- Access to gated community chats and technical leads
- Co-marketing opportunities around key activations
- Early input into integration paths

## Active workstreams

- **Partner intro video** — recordings from EthCC booth being edited by Gonçalo. Posted with full partner logo wall.
- **Community kick-off event** — technical spec walkthrough format, 2x 10-min focus sessions from the EEZ team plus space for Alliance members to present, share, or do research deep dives. Programmed with the community, not at it.
- **Ongoing comms** — Telegram group as the public channel, more focused working group channel anticipated 3–6 months out.
