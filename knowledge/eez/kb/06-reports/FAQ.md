# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is EEZ?

The Ethereum Economic Zone enables synchronous composability across L2s using real-time ZK proofs.

## How is it different from bridges?

| Aspect | Bridges | EEZ |
|--------|---------|-----|
| Execution | Async | Synchronous |
| Transactions | 2-3 | 1 |
| Security | Bridge risk | Ethereum |
| Finality | Eventually | Atomic |

## Who's behind EEZ?

**Founders:** Gnosis (Ernst, Köppelmann) + Zisk (Baylina)  
**Backers:** Ethereum Foundation  
**Partners:** 10 day-1 members

## When does EEZ launch?

- **Q2 2026:** Technical specs and benchmarks
- **Q3 2026:** First integrations begin
- **2026+:** Adoption accelerates

## Is EEZ centralized?

No. Governed by Swiss non-profit, open-source, multi-stakeholder.

## Will my L2 need to migrate?

Likely yes, for composability and liquidity access.

## What about Ethereum re-orgs?

L2s must follow re-orgs (rare, manageable complexity for atomic benefits).

## Why is this better than Cosmos/Polkadot?

EEZ has tighter Ethereum coupling and proven security model.
