# EEZ Comprehensive Report

Complete reference for the Ethereum Economic Zone.

---

## The Problem

**Ethereum Fragmentation:** 100+ isolated L2s with fragmented liquidity, bridging complexity, and poor UX for cross-L2 interactions.

---

## The Solution

**Real-Time ZK Proofs + Synchronous Composability**

EEZ enables atomic cross-L2 transactions using:
- Real-time zero-knowledge proving (Zisk technology)
- Proxy contract coordination
- Ethereum mainnet settlement
- No protocol-level changes required

---

## Vision: "One Ethereum"

Restore Ethereum as a coherent economic zone where L2s compose together seamlessly, liquidity consolidates, and Ethereum regains settlement layer dominance.
