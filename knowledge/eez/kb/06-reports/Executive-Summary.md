# Executive Summary

High-level overview of EEZ for decision-makers.

## Challenge

Ethereum has 100+ isolated L2s with fragmented liquidity and poor composability.

## Solution

Real-time ZK proofs enable synchronous composability across L2s.

## Key Benefits

- **Users:** Atomic transactions, no bridge slippage
- **Developers:** Write once, compose across L2s
- **Ethereum:** Regains settlement layer dominance

## Team & Backing

- **Founders:** Gnosis (Ernst, Köppelmann) + Zisk (Baylina)
