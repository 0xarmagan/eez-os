## Summary

Gnosis is an Ethereum-aligned infrastructure and product company, founded 2015, and one of the oldest active teams in the Ethereum ecosystem. Originally known for prediction markets (Gnosis Safe, Gnosis Protocol/CoW Protocol), it has evolved into a multi-product organization spanning a smart contract wallet (Safe), a DeFi DEX (CoW Protocol), a consumer payments product (Gnosis Pay), and now the EEZ infrastructure initiative.

## Role / Function

Co-initiator and lead sponsor of the EEZ initiative alongside Zisk. Gnosis provides organizational credibility, existing ecosystem relationships (Safe, CoW Swap are EEZ Alliance members), and the non-profit governance vehicle through its Swiss legal structure. Gnosis Chain (formerly xDAI) provides a working precedent for an ETH-aligned L2/sidechain.

## Notable products

- **Gnosis Safe** (now Safe) — the dominant multi-sig smart contract wallet in Ethereum
- **CoW Protocol** — intent-based DEX, protecting users from MEV
- **Gnosis Pay** — Visa card backed by on-chain assets (stablecoins)
- **Gnosis Chain** — ETH-aligned L1/sidechain (formerly xDAI), using ETH as native token

## Strategic positioning

Gnosis's full product stack — payments, DeFi, wallet, infrastructure — benefits directly from synchronous cross-chain composability. EEZ is not a neutral infrastructure bet for Gnosis; it is also a platform play that consolidates its products within a unified execution environment.
