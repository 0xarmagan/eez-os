## 🎯 Key Concepts at a Glance

**The Problem:** Ethereum has fragmentation, not a scaling problem.
- Each L2 is an isolated island with separate liquidity and bridges

**The Solution:** The Ethereum Economic Zone enables synchronous composability.
- Real-time zero-knowledge proofs enable atomic cross-chain transactions
- Unified liquidity and smart contract interactions across L2s
- Ethereum mainnet remains the settlement layer

**The Vision:** "One Ethereum"
- Restore Ethereum as a coherent economic zone
- Liquidity consolidates, composability becomes native
- Network effects strengthen Ethereum's dominance

**The Team:** Gnosis, Zisk, Ethereum Foundation + 10 founding partners
- Friederike Ernst & Martin Köppelmann (Gnosis)
- Jordi Baylina (Zisk)
- Alliance: Aave, Flashbots, Nethermind, Centrifuge, Safe, CoW Swap, Titan, Beaver Build, Monerium, xStocks

**The Timeline:** Announced March 29, 2026 at EthCC Cannes
- Gnosis Chain transitioning from L1 to native Ethereum L2
- Phased rollout of EEZ implementations
