## EEZ Governance

### Governance Structure
- **Entity:** Swiss non-profit (credibly neutral)
- **Stewards:** Founding partners + Gnosis leadership
- **Philosophy:** Open-source, transparent, decentralized decision-making

### Key Governance Principles
- ✅ Credibly neutral — no single entity controls EEZ
- ✅ Open-source — all software released publicly
- ✅ Multi-stakeholder — decisions involve all partners
- ✅ Transparent — governance processes documented

## Questions About Leadership

**Q: Is EEZ controlled by Gnosis?**  
A: No. Gnosis is leading development, but EEZ is governed as a credibly neutral, open-source initiative with a Swiss non-profit entity.

**Q: Can other L2s join EEZ?**  
A: Yes. Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, or any L2 can implement EEZ. Gnosis is the first, but not the only.

**Q: Who decides EEZ governance?**  
A: The 10 founding partners + Gnosis, through a transparent governance process (details TBD).
