# Open Intents Framework — Adoption Push Campaign

**Source:** Open Intents Framework (OIF) / LI.FI / Ethereum Foundation
**Date:** May 26, 2026
**Type:** Ecosystem-wide co-marketing campaign
**Original doc:** `Open Intents Framework - Adoption Push.pdf`

---

## What It Is

An ecosystem-wide X campaign launched by the Ethereum Foundation and LI.FI to mark the Open Intents Framework moving from concept to real adoption. Teams across Ethereum are asked to QT the EF manifesto post with their perspective on open and permissionless intents infrastructure.

**Tagline:** "A More Intentional Ethereum."

---

## Campaign Flow

1. LI.FI publishes article: "A More Intentional Ethereum: LI.FI Intents and the Open Intents Framework"
2. Ethereum Foundation QTs the article with an OIF manifesto (12:30 PM EST, May 26 from EF X account)
3. Ecosystem teams QT with their perspective on: open/permissionless intents, why shared standards matter, how they're contributing

Each team uses a customised branded asset (generator: https://intentionalethereum.vercel.app).

---

## OIF Comms Guidelines

- Ecosystem-first. No single team positioned as "owner" of intents infrastructure.
- Focus: collaboration, adoption, open standards, ecosystem alignment.

**Suggested themes:** open infrastructure, permissionless participation, shared standards, ecosystem coordination, intents adoption, builders building together.

---

## EEZ Relevance

EEZ's async path uses cross-chain intents (relayers) as one deployment model. EEZ's core differentiator — synchronous composability — is the execution layer that gives intents their full power. The right framing: intents are the user-intent layer, EEZ is the synchronous execution layer they resolve into.

**Participation decision:** See `outputs/drafts/alliance-outreach-OIF-co-marketing-2026-05-24.md` for the full brief, QT copy drafts, and action plan.

**Comms alignment:**
- OIF's "no single team as owner" = EEZ's "we, not it" alliance pillar. High alignment.
- OIF's "permissionless, open standards" = EEZ's credibly neutral, Ethereum-governed framing. High alignment.
- OIF's "intents" framing vs EEZ's "synchronous composability" — need careful bridging. EEZ is the execution layer, not an intents routing protocol.
