# EEZ Coalition Charter: Partner Communications Scope

**v1.0 · 2026-05-30 · Internal + Partner**

---

## 1. What this is

This charter defines what EEZ Alliance partners can say about EEZ in their own communications: blog posts, X threads, conference decks, press releases, and any other content published under the partner's name.

**Scope:** Partner outbound communications about EEZ. What you can say, what needs review, and what you cannot say.

**What this does NOT cover:** Who is authorized to speak as an EEZ spokesperson, which EEZ team members can make which claims, or how EEZ's own channels are governed. Those rules live in [EEZ Brand Governance Rules](EEZ-Brand-Governance-Rules.md).

**Owner:** Armagan Ercan
**Review contact:** armagan.ercan@gnosis.io

---

## 2. What partners can say without review

The following claims are approved for partner use in any format, without prior review. Use them as written or adapt them in your own voice, provided the substance does not change.

### Membership claims

- "[Your protocol] is a member of the EEZ Alliance."
- "[Your protocol] joined the EEZ Alliance in [month/year]."
- "[Your protocol] is a founding member of the EEZ Alliance." (only if this is accurate)
- "[Your protocol] participates in the EEZ Alliance."
- "We are part of a growing coalition of protocols building toward synchronous composability on Ethereum."

### Zone and protocol claims

- "EEZ is a synchronous composability layer for Ethereum rollups."
- "EEZ enables atomic transactions across participating rollups."
- "EEZ is governed by a Swiss non-profit foundation."
- "EEZ has no token."
- "EEZ is fully open source."
- "EEZ is co-funded by the Ethereum Foundation."

### Thesis and motivation claims

These describe your own reasons for joining and your view of the problem EEZ addresses. They require no review:

- "[Your protocol] joined EEZ because we believe Ethereum's rollup fragmentation creates real UX and liquidity problems."
- "We see synchronous composability as necessary infrastructure for Ethereum to function as a unified financial layer."
- "Participating in EEZ is part of how [your protocol] contributes to Ethereum alignment."
- "We build with the EEZ zone standard in mind."
- "EEZ zone compatibility is part of our technical direction."

## 3. What requires Armagan's review before publishing

Send a draft to armagan.ercan@gnosis.io before publishing if your content includes any of the following.

**Standard turnaround: 48 hours. Time-sensitive requests (conference in less than 48 hours, breaking news cycle): same-day. Flag urgency in the subject line.**

### Trigger conditions

- Any claim about EEZ proving speed, benchmark figures, or ZK performance
- Any specific mainnet or testnet timeline ("EEZ launches in Q3," "testnet expected this summer")
- Any statement characterizing EEZ's technical capabilities beyond the approved claims in Section 2
- Any content that uses a named co-founder or spokesperson (Friederike Ernst, Martin Köppelmann, Jordi Baylina) to support a claim
- Any statement that could read as speaking for EEZ rather than about EEZ
- Any content that references Vitalik Buterin, the Ethereum Foundation, or other external parties in relation to EEZ
- Any co-marketing or joint announcement with EEZ branding (separate from the EEZ team's own announcement coordination)
- Any statement about EEZ governance mechanics, phase roadmap, or protocol design decisions beyond what is publicly documented
- Anything that makes EEZ membership sound exclusive or conditional on the partner's own product

When in doubt, send it. Review protects both sides.

## 4. What partners cannot say

| Claim | Why it is out of scope |
|---|---|
| "Vitalik endorses EEZ" or any variation implying personal endorsement | Vitalik's co-presence at EEZ-adjacent events reflects technical alignment and shared values, not a personal endorsement. EEZ never claims EF or individual endorsement beyond what EF has publicly stated. |
| "EEZ is backed by Gnosis" or "Gnosis's EEZ" | Gnosis co-initiated EEZ and contributes resources, but EEZ is not a Gnosis product. It is governed by a Swiss non-profit foundation. "EEZ, a project supported by Gnosis" is acceptable only in contexts where that fact is directly relevant. |
| "EEZ is governed by Ethereum today" or "Ethereum fully governs EEZ" | GnosisDAO governance operates via Snapshot (off-chain). Full on-chain governance is Phase 3 work and has not launched. Claiming full Ethereum governance today is factually inaccurate. |
| "We are an EEZ zone" or "our rollup is an EEZ zone" | No zones are deployed on Ethereum mainnet. Calling your rollup an EEZ zone is a premature claim that misstates current deployment status. |
| Claiming EEZ membership as exclusive or as a competitive gate | EEZ is an open framework. Membership is participation, not a gate. Framing EEZ membership as conferring exclusivity misrepresents the protocol's design intent. |
| Speaking as an EEZ spokesperson or in EEZ's institutional voice | Alliance members speak for themselves about their own participation. Speaking as EEZ (e.g., "EEZ is excited to announce") is reserved for authorized EEZ personnel. See EEZ Brand Governance Rules for the authorized spokesperson list. |
| Any claim about EEZ's token, points, airdrop, or future incentive mechanism | EEZ has no token. There is no points program, airdrop, or retroactive reward mechanism. Making or implying any such claim contradicts a core structural commitment of the protocol. |
| Technical performance claims not drawn from a published Zisk benchmark | Benchmark figures require citation of the specific hardware configuration and conditions from the published Zisk report. Unstated or approximate figures misrepresent the current evidence base. |

## 5. Alliance mark usage

### Where the mark can be displayed

- Your website's partner, integration, or ecosystem page
- Conference decks and presentations, in a partner/supporter context
- Blog posts and announcements about your EEZ Alliance membership
- Press releases announcing your membership

The alliance mark always links to **eez.io/alliance** when displayed in a digital context.

### Where the mark cannot be displayed

- In contexts that imply EEZ endorses your product, service, or claims
- Alongside claims in Section 4 of this charter
- In content that has not received required review per Section 3
- In token sale, fundraising, or investment materials without explicit written approval from Armagan

### When membership lapses

Display rights for the alliance mark end at the point membership lapses. Remove the mark from all active surfaces within 30 days of lapse. Archived content (e.g., a dated blog post announcing original membership) does not need to be altered.
