# EEZ Public Brief

**What this is:** A factual reference for journalists, researchers, and builders encountering EEZ for the first time.
**Last updated:** 2026-05-30

---

## What EEZ is

EEZ (Ethereum Economic Zone) is an open-source framework and governance standard for Ethereum-native economic zones.

Any team can build a zone using the EEZ framework. There is no application, no committee, no permission required. A zone that implements the EEZ technical standard is an EEZ zone. The EEZ Alliance is a separate, voluntary coordination layer — teams who want to participate in the broader ecosystem, signal alignment publicly, and use the alliance mark join it. Building with EEZ does not require joining the alliance.

**EEZ is not:**
- An L2 or a new chain
- A company or foundation
- A token project
- A permissioned system — the framework is open source

**EEZ is:**
- An open-source standard anyone can implement
- A governance framework grounded in Ethereum's existing processes
- A voluntary alliance of teams coordinating around the standard

---

## What problem it solves

Ethereum's rollup ecosystem solved throughput. It created a fragmentation problem in return.

Assets and contracts live on separate chains. Transactions that span chains require bridges. Composability, one of Ethereum's core properties, breaks at the L2 boundary. Users and protocols manage this friction manually.

EEZ addresses this through zones that are governed and settled by Ethereum rather than by independent teams or foundations. Zones are not standalone chains. They are defined areas of Ethereum's economic surface, with standard governance rules and verifiable settlement.

The goal is composability that does not require trust assumptions at every boundary.

## What is live and what is not

**Live:**
- EEZ Alliance: coalition of independent teams (block builders, ZK provers, DeFi protocols) coordinating on the standard
- Technical specification: in active research and review
- GnosisDAO governance: EEZ coordination operates under GnosisDAO's governance process

**Not yet live:**
- Zones on Ethereum mainnet
- Full on-chain verifiable governance end-to-end
- EEZ Alliance mark (the credential partners display on their own properties)
- Zone-specific developer tooling

EEZ is in the thesis and specification phase. There is no product to deploy today.
