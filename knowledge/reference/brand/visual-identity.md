# Use of the Icon

## The Owl

The owl is the Gnosis brand symbol. It carries deliberate meaning:

- **Knowledge and watchfulness** — the "wise old owl"
- **Nocturnal** — sees in the dark, having insight where others don't
- **360° awareness** — vigilance, all-seeing observation
- **Mysticism** — symbol of "what is hidden"
- **Guardian** — protects without dominating
- **Stealth** — silent, precise, never loud

The owl isn't hiding in the shadows — present, sometimes in the center, sometimes in unexpected places, always watching. A playful guardian who sees what others don't and hands power back to the people.
