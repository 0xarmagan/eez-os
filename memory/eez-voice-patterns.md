# EEZ Voice Patterns

## Position-first framing

Lead with EEZ's position on the topic, not with the endorsement.

Wrong: "We're excited to support OIF."
Right: "Every proprietary intent protocol is just another island. OIF is building it right."

The position names the problem. The endorsement follows from the position. Never reverse this order.

## Canonical vocabulary

Use these terms exactly. They are not interchangeable with synonyms.

- "reverse fragmentation" — not "fix fragmentation" or "solve fragmentation"; "reverse" implies undoing damage, not applying a patch
- "One Ethereum" — the mission statement; use as-is
- "not a hundred islands" — the fragmentation framing in plain terms
- "synchronous composability" — the technical differentiator; do not substitute "interoperability"
- "same block, no bridges" — what synchronous composability means in plain English
- "credibly neutral" — governance framing; do not paraphrase

## What to avoid

- "fix fragmentation" — too patch-like, undercuts the scale of the claim
- "unlock value" — banned phrase across all EEZ and Gnosis content
- "next-generation", "game-changer", "world-class" — also banned
- Partner language borrowing: if the partner's tagline is their line, find EEZ's equivalent position in EEZ's words
- Campaign-speak that could apply to any project: if you could swap "EEZ" for any other name and it still reads fine, rewrite it

## X thread structure rules

- Short sentences. One idea per sentence.
- No bullet lists in tweets.
- No em dashes anywhere.
- First tweet: EEZ's stance or the problem. Do not open with an endorsement.
- One CTA at the end only.
- Default length: 5 tweets. Up to 10-15 for visual deep-dives with assets.
- No headers or bullet structure in threads -- human touch, not structured prose.

## Register test

Ask: does this sound like a core dev on All Core Devs or a campaign press release? Matter-of-fact, builder-credible, no hype. If it sounds like a press release, rewrite it.

## Ecosystem campaign participation

When EEZ joins a campaign as a QT participant:
- The branded asset carries the brand identity. The tweet carries the voice.
- Express EEZ's genuine position on the topic -- do not echo the campaign's own language back at them.
- Check whether EEZ/Gnosis is already listed as a named supporter; this shifts framing from endorsing to participating.
